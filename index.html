How to Play

The main game is a word-based challenge where you interact with various themes. Your objective is to find or guess words that are related to the chosen theme.

The Goal

The ultimate goal of the game is to solve the Word Challenge puzzles for each theme. The more words you find, the higher your score and the more you progress!

Tips & Tricks

  • Always check the timer on the wheel button to know when your next free spin is ready.
  • Save your ad-spins for when you're stuck on a particularly difficult word challenge!
  • Try out all the different themes to keep the game fun and challenging.
